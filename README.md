Biography
======

Dr. Qibang Liu is a Research Scientist at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA) at the University of Illinois Urbana‚ÄìChampaign and an Adjunct Assistant Professor at Kansas State University. His research focuses on advancing state-of-the-art numerical algorithms and AI-driven methods, leveraging high-performance and AI computing to solve multiscale, multiphysics scientific and engineering problems. He develops next-generation tools that enable breakthroughs in materials science, advanced manufacturing, and structural engineering. His core interests include scientific machine learning (SciML), AI-aided engineering (A2E), multiphysics simulation, and computational mechanics.

Research Interests
======
SciML, A2E (AI-aided Engineering), Multiphysics Simulation, Computational Mechanics, Peridynamics, Advanced Manufacturing
